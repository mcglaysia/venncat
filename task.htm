The Task

  1. Each crewmember completes 4 independent journals.
  2. Crewmembers will research their explorer using books, encyclopedias and Internet resources.
  3. Crewmembers will present their findings in the form of a Power Point presentation.
  4. Throughout the project crewmembers need to demonstrate and evaluate their ability to work cooperatively.

Job Descriptions:

 Recorders: research and record basic information about the expedition, including the purpose, origin, destination and discoveries.

 Engineer: research the supplies and transportation used for the expedition and create a diagram of the transportation used.

 Navigator/Cartographer: research the navigational equipment used during the expedition and create a map showing the route. 

Interpreter: research the natives encountered and the environment of the new land.
